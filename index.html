About Me

Hello, my name is Jani Jon Lumibao, and I graduated from the University of Central Florida with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering. I am a freelance web developer passionate about building clean, responsive, and user-focused websites using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and database technologies.

In addition to web technologies, I am deeply interested in both software engineering and embedded hardware systems. My engineering background, combined with strong creative interests in dance and digital media, enables me to build solutions that are both technically sound and visually engaging.
